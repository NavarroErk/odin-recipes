Sandwich


Description: This is a sandwich

Required Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Steps to make a sandwich:

  1. Grab two slices of bread and put them on a plate
  2. Place an ample amount of lettuce onto one slice of bread
  3. Place as many tomato slices as desired atop of the lettuce
  4. Place two slices of cheese on top of the tomato slices
  5. Grab 3 slices of ham, roll them up, and place them on top of the cheese
  6. Finally, put the other slice of bread on top to complete the sandwich
